User Guide

In the era of mobile Internet, mobile phone users have a large amount of audio, pictures, videos and other materials that need to be created, beautified, classified and collected, and pursue a beautiful music album or video album through simple operations.

1. Add a picture Select the images in the phone; select the images upload, and then click the corresponding images to adjust the order.

2. Add music Add default music on the software, and one video album supports up to one music.

3. Add a title to the video A complete video music album work that generally contains a video title. Music album app supports free to add the title of the video for each work.

4. Submit the production After uploading all the materials and setting the parameters, you can submit the work to the app.
